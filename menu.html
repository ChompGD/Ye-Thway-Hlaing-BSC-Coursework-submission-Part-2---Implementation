ABOUT SQUARE JUMP

Welcome to Square Jump! In this game the player will have to jump over the various obstacles that moves along the way. All the player needs to do is to just click the mouse on the right time, nothing else to do! There will be 5 different stages the player will have to conquer, and each stage will progressively get harder and harder, and different gameplay gimmicks and mechanics will be introduced. Good luck and have fun playing Square Jump :3
